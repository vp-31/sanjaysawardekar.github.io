माझी कहाणी · My Story

३८ वर्षांचा प्रवास — एका तरुण अधिकाऱ्यापासून विभागीय व्यवस्थापकापर्यंत

Sanjay Tanaji Sawardekar was born on January 1, 1965, in Maharashtra. A commerce graduate (B.Com, BGL), he joined the Life Insurance Corporation of India as a young officer in the mid-1980s — and never looked back.

"विमा म्हणजे फक्त पैसा नाही — तो आहे कुटुंबाच्या भविष्याची सुरक्षा."

Over nearly four decades, Sanjay rose through every rank. He served as Divisional Manager, overseeing operations that touched thousands of policyholders. His deep understanding of LIC's inner workings — from policy issuance to claim settlement — made him a go-to resource for colleagues across Maharashtra.

His crowning achievement came when he was appointed Principal of the Division Training Center, LIC of India. In this role, he trained and mentored hundreds of LIC officers and agents, shaping the next generation of insurance professionals. The Fellowship of Insurance Institute of India (FIII) — the highest insurance qualification in the country — recognized his exceptional expertise.

Today, having retired from active service, Sanjay continues his mission as an LIC Advisor based in Mulund, Mumbai. He sells new LIC policies and services existing ones — not as a salesperson, but as a trusted family advisor who brings 38 years of institutional knowledge to every conversation.

He speaks Marathi, Hindi, and English fluently, ensuring every client — from a young professional buying their first term plan to a retired couple managing their pension — feels heard, understood, and guided.
